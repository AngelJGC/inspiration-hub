## Techniques

- **Page-transition "slab" overlay** — fixed div + GSAP timeline orchestrated by a context provider.
- **Sticky section stacking** with gradual border-radius reveal (tiles round off into each other).
- **Projects grid image trail on pointer move** — `requestAnimationFrame` queue.
- **Marquee** with infinite translate measured from `getBoundingClientRect`.
