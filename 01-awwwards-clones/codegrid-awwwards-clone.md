# CodeGrid-com/awwwards-clone

> **Direct clone of the Awwwards homepage itself.** Understand the editorial grid behind every
> SOTD showcase.

- **URL:** https://github.com/CodeGrid-com/awwwards-clone
- **Live:** https://awwwards-clone-codegrid.vercel.app
- **Stars:** ⭐ 300+
- **Stack:** Next.js · GSAP · Lenis

## Techniques

- **Hero video masked by animated SVG clip-path** that opens on scroll (keyhole reveal).
- **Project cards with cursor-tracked zoomed preview** in a fixed frame (hover a card → fixed
  image in corner updates smoothly).
- **Horizontal scroll gallery** — `ScrollTrigger.create({ pin, scrub })`.

## Why inspiring

When the user says "make it feel like **Awwwards itself**", this is the literal rebuild of that
site. Good meta-reference for studying how the aggregator that showcases the best work
structures its own UI.
