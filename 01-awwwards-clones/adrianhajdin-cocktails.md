# adrianhajdin/gsap_cocktails

> **Awwwards-caliber beverage-brand clone.** Great SplitText reference.

- **URL:** https://github.com/adrianhajdin/gsap_cocktails
- **Stars:** ⭐ 300+ (approx)
- **Stack:** React · Vite · Tailwind · GSAP (SplitText, ScrollTrigger)

## Techniques worth stealing

- **SplitText character reveals** — per-letter stagger on hero headline, scroll-scrubbed.
- **Parallax product bottles** — foreground glass at a slower scroll rate than the background.
- **Custom horizontal carousel** with GSAP Observer (scroll + drag).
- **Scroll-pinned hero** that releases only when text completes its reveal.
- **Fluid-scale typography** — `clamp()`-based responsive hero H1.

## Why inspiring

Best starting point specifically for **beverage / food / hospitality** brand landings. The
SplitText choreography is cleaner than most tutorials because it's scrubbed (not just
"play-on-enter"), making it feel tied to the user's scroll.

## Used in this showcase

- `06r-obsidiana` manifesto section — Spylt-style kinetic typography uses this SplitText +
  scrub recipe.
