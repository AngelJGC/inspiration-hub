> **Focused on the "TikTok-viral" microinteractions currently winning SOTDs.**

- **Stars:** ⭐ 200+
- **Stack:** Next.js 14 · TypeScript · Framer Motion · GSAP

## Techniques

- **Text reveal** — per-character `motion.span` with staggered Y transforms.
- **Bento-grid layout** with `layoutId` shared transitions (clicking a tile expands it into a detail view).
- **Scroll-snapped horizontal section** — CSS `scroll-snap-type` + inner flex container.

## Why inspiring

Tom's YouTube recipes are the hit-list of 2024-2026 Awwwards-y microinteractions. Every tile in
a modern bento grid, every "card expands into full-screen" transition — he's got the ~50-line
version.
