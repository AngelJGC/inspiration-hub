# github.com/topics/awwwards-inspired

> **A GitHub topic index, not a single repo.** When you need "something Awwwards-y" and don't
> know exactly what yet, start here.

- **URL:** https://github.com/topics/awwwards-inspired?o=desc&s=stars
- **Contains:** Dozens of Ochi Design, k72.ca, Cartier, Apple, and other SOTD rebuilds.

## Recurring archetypes under this topic

### Ochi Design clones
- **Stack:** Next.js · Framer Motion · GSAP · Locomotive Scroll
- **Signatures:** Horizontal hover-video grid, bold kinetic type, cursor follower with "Click →"
  label, full-page expanding thumbnail transitions.

### k72.ca-inspired
- **Stack:** Next.js · GSAP · Lenis
- **Signatures:** Smooth scroll + kinetic typography + page-transition overlays, minimalist
  layouts, monochrome palette with one saturated accent.

### Cartier / luxury brand clones
- **Stack:** React · GSAP · Three.js
- **Signatures:** Slow reveal, floating product photography, refined serif type, marquee
  heritage-dates.

## Why this topic matters

Awwwards SOTD is the top of the motion-design pyramid; this topic is how the open-source
community tracks which sites got rebuilt. Sort by stars, open the top 5, read the top 5
READMEs — you'll have a mental library of 2025-2026 motion patterns in under an hour.

## Usage tip

When starting a new landing, **spawn a parallel research agent** to scrape the top 10 repos
under this topic and summarize their README Tech Stacks + screenshots. You'll instantly see
which patterns are hot right now.
