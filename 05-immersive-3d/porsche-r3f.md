# GeethoshGv/React-Three-Fiber (Porsche)

> **Showroom feel for a Porsche; small code, big visual payoff.**

- **URL:** https://github.com/GeethoshGv/React-Three-Fiber
- **Stack:** React · React Three Fiber · drei

## Techniques worth stealing

- **HDRI environment lighting** via drei `<Environment preset="city" />` — instant photoreal.
- **Scroll-driven camera dolly** — camera moves around the car on scroll.
- **Interactive orbit controls** with damping enabled.
- **Reflective car paint** — `MeshPhysicalMaterial` with high `clearcoat` and `roughness` tuning.

## Why inspiring

Minimal code, maximal wow. Under 500 lines. Good reference for any **single-product hero**
where the user wants one beautiful object at center stage.
