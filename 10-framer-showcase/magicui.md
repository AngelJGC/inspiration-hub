# magicuidesign/magicui

> **The cheat sheet for "Framer component libraries in 2025".**

- **URL:** https://github.com/magicuidesign/magicui
- **Live:** https://magicui.design
- **Stars:** ⭐ 13k+
- **Stack:** Next.js · TypeScript · Tailwind · Framer Motion

## Techniques (50+ prebuilt components)

- **Orbiting Circles** — elements rotating around a central point.
- **Animated Beam** — combined SVG paths + Framer for "connection line" effects between two DOM
  nodes (the LangChain-logo-to-Logo connector look).
- **Ripple, Meteors, Spotlight** — ambient motion backgrounds.
- **Performance-minded** — `will-change` + `transform3d` applied consistently.
- **Variants driven by `useInView`** with `once: true` defaults.

## Why inspiring

Every block is documented, live-demoed, and drops into Next.js App Router with one copy-paste.
When the user says "like LangChain's landing page" or "like Resend / Vercel aesthetic", magicui
is 80% of the answer.
