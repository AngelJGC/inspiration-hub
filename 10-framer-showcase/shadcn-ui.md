# shadcn-ui/ui

> **Not marketed as an animation repo**, but encodes some of the cleanest Framer Motion patterns
> you'll find. Copy the components and the motion comes along.

- **URL:** https://github.com/shadcn-ui/ui
- **Live:** https://ui.shadcn.com
- **Stars:** ⭐ 75k+
- **Stack:** Next.js 15 · Radix UI · Tailwind · Framer Motion

## Techniques

- **Command menu** with `layout` animations and keyboard focus rings (⌘K pattern).
- **Sheet/drawer gestures** — `drag` + `dragConstraints` for iOS-style pulls.
- **Animated tab indicator** using a shared `layoutId` bar that slides between tabs.
- **Toast queue** powered by `AnimatePresence` + `mode="popLayout"`.

## Why inspiring

shadcn is the **de-facto UI kit of 2024-2026 for Next.js**. But beyond the components, it's a
masterclass in restrained, composable Framer motion. Every animation earns its place — nothing
is gratuitous. Read `components/ui/sheet.tsx` and `dialog.tsx` as animation tutorials.
