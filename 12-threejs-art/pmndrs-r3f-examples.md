## Example categories

- **GPGPU boids / flocking** with compute shaders.
- **Scroll-tied sticky camera rigs** using drei `<ScrollControls>`.
- **Instanced particles (10k+)** without frame drops.
- **CSG (constructive solid geometry)** demos — boolean operations on meshes.

## Why inspiring

When someone asks "can you do X in R3F?" — this is where you look first. Most of the listed
examples are **CodeSandbox / live demos with source**, so you can fork and iterate in seconds.

## How to use

Open the examples index, pick 3 that match the brief, fork in CodeSandbox, combine. You'll be
80% of the way to a custom scene before writing a line of your own code.
