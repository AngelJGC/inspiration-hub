# AdityaAnand1/Crypto-Landing-Page

> **Small, readable codebase.** Great for extracting a single "floating coin hero" pattern.

- **URL:** https://github.com/AdityaAnand1/Crypto-Landing-Page
- **Live:** https://crypto-landing-page-adityaanand1.vercel.app
- **Stars:** ⭐ 100+
- **Stack:** React · Styled Components · Framer Motion

## Techniques

- **Coin 3D float effect** — combined `rotateX`/`rotateY` springs driven by pointer.
- **Responsive breakpoints** via styled-components template literals.
- **Section-by-section `AnimatePresence` with `mode="wait"`** for route-like transitions.

## Why inspiring

Minimal footprint. When you need just **one floating/rotating crypto asset in a hero** without
pulling in Three.js, this is the spring+tilt recipe.
